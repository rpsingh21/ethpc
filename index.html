About us

The aim and objective of ETHC 2K17 is to provide a platform for researcher, engineers, academicians as well as industrial professional from all of india to present their research result and development activities in many scientific and engineering problem . In present scenario we have witnessed a remarkable development in high performance computing technology and this process likely to continue . This conference provides opportunities for the delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face , to face ,to establish business or research relations and to find global partner as CSI student chapter FGIET submitted conference paper will be reviewed by technical committee of the conference . English is the official language of the conferences .we welcome original technical research paper of maximum pages at IEEE electronic (DOC or PDF) format through the email ethpc17@fgiet.ac.in .
